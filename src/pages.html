Components Patterns Pages

Pages

Pages are the highest level of the ecosystem. They take patterns and components and fill them with context-specific data and real copy.

User Dashboard

The landing screen containing the user's specific metrics, recent activity, and quick actions.

Product Detail Page (PDP)

The specific page for an item, rendering product images, reviews, and pricing.

Pricing Page

The public-facing page showcasing subscription tiers, feature tables, and FAQs.

User Profile Page

The screen where an individual edits their personal bio, email, and avatar.

404

404 Error Page

A standalone full-screen layout handling a broken link destination.

Analytics Reports Screen

A data-heavy page displaying specific charts, logs, and export options.
